Our Story

Astra Lex Partners is a full-service law firm providing exceptional corporate, litigation, and technology legal services with a distinctly business-oriented approach.

We believe that great legal counsel is not merely reactive — it is anticipatory, strategic, and deeply integrated with each client's long-term ambitions. Our attorneys bring decades of collective expertise across Indonesia's most complex commercial landscapes.

From emerging startups to established multinationals, we are committed to delivering counsel that is both rigorous in its legal foundation and astute in its commercial reality.
